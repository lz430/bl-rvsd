HeartSmart.com is proud to be a leading provider of Automated External Defibrillators, also referred to as AEDs. We offer AED devices by industry leading AED manufacturers as well as the supplies, services, training and support needed to ensure your AED and AED program is thought-out, maintained, and your responders are trained and ready.

We base our company's reputation on providing the best AEDs at the best prices in the industry. Along with the best AEDs, we offer and provide essential services, support and training that make HeartSmart.com your one-stop shop for purchasing an AED or maintaining an AED.
